WHAT WE DO

Cuba is a unique place for doing business. We create - and manage - media & digital content for businesses exporting from Cuba or marketing themselves in a Cuban context.

We make things happen. Living in Havana & having local Cubans within our team means we have the network & know-how to navigate the country's 'unique' bureaucracy & informal ways of getting things done.

Cuba is changing rapidly & the future is one of excitement & optimism. Be part of that opportunity.

MADE IN HAVANA

WHY NOW

The normalising of relations with the US last year created a real buzz around Cuba.

Obama, The Rolling Stones, fashion house Chanel & artist Diplo have all touched down on Cuban soil in 2016.

Cuba's infamous isolation has become its main asset: a 'time-warp' endowed with an authenticity & uniqueness that fascinates the rest of the world.

It provides a novel context for any media content.
